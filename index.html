Save Instructions

Press [1], [2], or [3] to navigate save files.
Press [S] to save your game into that slot.
Press [L] to load your game from that slot.
Press [U] to Undo and [R] to Redo.

Game Instructions

Use Arrow Keys to move player on grid. Every player step passes time.
Press [SPACEBAR] to sow or reap plants in each cell.
Plants need neighbors to grow.
